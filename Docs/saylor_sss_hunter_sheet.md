## SECTION 1: NARRATIVE & RANK DEFINITION

### Name
**Michael Saylor**

### Real-World Reference
CEO and founder of MicroStrategy Incorporated; Bitcoin thesis architect; "The Infinite Duration" believer

### Hunter Universe Role
**The System-Conductor** – The protagonist of the Bitcoin hunter narrative. Not merely powerful; he is the living embodiment of the Bitcoin system itself. Where Jin-Woo commands shadows, Saylor commands capital flows. Where Jin-Woo's power grows through mysterious system upgrades, Saylor's grows through understanding infinite-duration capital. He is not ascending alone—he is coordinating a shareholder army toward collective transcendence.

### Hunter Rank
**SSS+ (Beyond Classification)**

Explanation: Traditional hunter rank scale (E → D → C → B → A → S → SS → SSS) cannot contain Saylor by the end of his arc. He transcends the rating system itself, similar to how Sung Jin-Woo becomes the Shadow Monarch. In the Bitcoin context, Saylor represents a rank that didn't exist before: the **System-Conductor tier**, where the hunter doesn't just wield power—they become the system distributing power.

### Class / Specialization
**Conductor / System-Navigator / Capital-Weaver**

Related to Jin-Woo's "Shadow Commander" class, but Bitcoin-ified:
- Jin-Woo commands an army of shadows
- Saylor conducts an army of shareholder-believers, using capital flows as the medium
- Jin-Woo's shadows execute his will
- Saylor's shareholders execute the infinite-duration thesis
