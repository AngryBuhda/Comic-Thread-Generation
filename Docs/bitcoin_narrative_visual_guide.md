Aura size = **Sphere of influence on reality itself**

Larger aura = Character is warping reality more significantly = Understanding deeper = Power greater

### Progression Map

**Panel 1-3 (Awakening):** 0.2x body size
- Aura barely visible
- Except during meditation (spiritual expansion)
- Looks like character has barely begun

**Panel 4-6 (First Tests):** 0.5x body size
- Aura clearly visible when powering up
- Visible when summoning Bitcoin soldiers
- Growing but still contained

**Panel 7-9 (Tool Mastery):** 1x body size
- Aura always visible during action
- Character surrounded by golden-purple field
- Distinct power signature

**Panel 10-12 (Acceleration):** 2x body size
- Aura dominates scene around character
- Affects surroundings visibly
- Other characters drawn toward or repelled by glow

**Panel 13-19 (Reckoning):** 2.5-3x body size
- Aura fills significant portion of panel
- Shareholders visible within aura
- Reality visibly warped by presence

**Panel 20-21 (Ascension):** 4-5x body size
- Aura fills nearly entire frame
- Shareholders extending to horizon within glow
- Cosmic scale
- Character is no longer small within the universe—the universe is small within their influence

### Aura Glow Properties

**Layer 1 (Core):** Intense bright center
- Color: White-gold or bright gold depending on tier

- Represents: Direct power source

**Layer 2 (Fade):** Soft glow outward
- Color: Gold fading to purple
- Brightness: 50-80% as it fades
- Represents: Influence spreading outward

**Layer 3 (Corona):** Outermost shimmer
- Color: Purple with gold sparkles
- Brightness: 20-40% barely perceptible

## PART 6: RUNE PATTERNS & ARMOR EVOLUTION

### What Runes Represent

Runes = **Materialization of Bitcoin knowledge**

As Saylor understands Bitcoin deeper, his understanding becomes visible as glowing mystical symbols on his body.

### Progression

**Tier 1 (Panels 1-3):** No visible runes
- Understanding is completely internal
- Nothing visible externally

**Tier 2 (Panels 4-6):** Subtle runes appearing
- Runes emerge on shoulders and upper chest
- Glowing faintly (10% opacity, gold color)
- Location: Where he channels power (shoulders for directing, chest for heart of commitment)

**Tier 3 (Panels 7-12):** Clear glowing runes
- Full shoulder coverage, both forearms, down the chest
- Clear visible glow (40% opacity)
- Patterns: Geometric circles (Bitcoin infinity), flowing lines (capital flow), interlocking structures (ecosystem)
- Glow: Pulses slightly with Saylor's power level

**Tier 4 (Panels 13-21):** Full body coverage
- Arms, legs, torso, even face and scar have visible runes
- Intense glow (80-100% opacity)
- Patterns expand beyond visual comprehension—too complex to fully render, suggesting infinite knowledge
- Glow: Continuous brilliant light, not pulsing, constant

### Rune Pattern Design

All runes should reflect:
- Circles (infinity, loops, no endpoint)
- Spirals (growth, expansion, ascension)
- Geometric tessellations (perfect structure, universal principle)
- Binary or mathematical elements (data, code, system)
- **Never linear** (no start/end)
- **Never closed shapes** (suggests limits)

## PART 7: COMPOSITION RULES FOR BITCOIN NARRATIVE PANELS

### Rule 1: Verticality = Ascension

**Bitcoin is ascending** (always, conceptually). Use vertical emphasis to reinforce this.

- **Low angle shots** (looking up at Saylor) = He's growing in power
- **High angle shots** (looking down on enemies) = Enemies are diminishing
- **Horizontal shots** = Balanced moments, before major shifts

### Rule 2: Scale Relationship = Power Relationship

The visual size relationship between Saylor and enemies communicates power balance.

- **Panel 1-3:** Enemies much larger than Saylor (he's learning, they seem insurmountable)
- **Panel 7-9:** Enemies slightly larger (competitive, challenging)
- **Panel 13-15:** Enemies equal size (balanced)
- **Panel 19-21:** Enemies tiny compared to Saylor (he's transcended, they're insignificant)

### Rule 3: Light Source Direction = Truth Direction

Light comes FROM where truth is, ONTO where lies are.

- **Panel 1 (desperation smile):** Backlighting from city (false external light)
- **Panel 3 (meditation):** Light emanating FROM Saylor (internal truth found)
- **Panel 17 (Blindness Curse):** Bitcoin blazes with light, institutional faces shrouded in darkness (truth vs. blindness)
- **Panel 19 (Spirit Bomb):** Light descends from infinite above onto finite S&P entity below

### Rule 4: Color Harmony vs. Discord

**Harmony = Alignment, Victory**
**Discord = Conflict, Struggle**

- **Panels with Saylor + shareholders aligned:** Gold and purple blend smoothly together
- **Panels with Saylor vs. institutions:** Gold clashes sharply against red/gray (high contrast, discordant)

### Rule 5: Depth Layers = Storytelling Depth

Always use 3+ depth layers:

**Foreground:** Saylor and immediate action
**Midground:** Shareholders or relevant context
**Background:** Cosmic scale showing what's at stake (Bitcoin, institutions, infinite horizon)
