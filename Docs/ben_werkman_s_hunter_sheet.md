## SECTION 1: NARRATIVE & RANK DEFINITION

### Name
**Ben Werkman**

### Real-World Reference
Strive Guild member; Bitcoin community support specialist; focused on building sustainable community infrastructure

### Hunter Universe Role
**The Healer-Commander** – Ben Werkman is the embodiment of strategic support and team resilience. Where Saylor conducts the infinite system, Matt Cole mobilizes believers, and Phon Le executes operations, Ben ensures the collective stays healthy and optimized. He is not a frontline fighter—he is the anchor that keeps the team standing. His power comes not from personal combat strength, but from amplifying and protecting the entire community. Bitcoin's victory is not possible without someone ensuring the team never breaks.

### Hunter Rank
**S-Rank (Same tier as Matt Cole, different specialty)**

Explanation: Ben Werkman is an elite S-Rank hunter, equal in rank to Matt Cole but with fundamentally different power expression. Where Matt is combat-focused guild leader, Ben is support-focused team anchor. His ranking reflects his value: a healer who can maintain entire armies in optimal condition is as crucial as a warrior leading charges. Both are S-Rank. Both are essential.

### Class / Specialization
**Healer / Support-Commander / Light-Weaver**

Derived from Min Byung-Gu's "Healer/Support" class but evolved:
- Min: Heals teammates, provides buffs, scales damage with HP
- Ben: Heals shareholder army, provides Bitcoin thesis stability, scales resilience with conviction
- Min: Light element healing magic
- Ben: "Bitcoin Light" (conviction-powered healing, stabilization)
- Both embody: Support that prevents failure through resilience and amplification

### Guild Affiliation
**Strive Guild (Support Specialist / Team Anchor)**

Official Title: Head of Community Resilience / Support Coordinator

## SECTION 2: VISUAL DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### OVERALL DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Ben Werkman's design differs from all previous characters in intentional ways:

**Saylor = Cosmic transcendence (lone visionary, expanding power)**  
**Phon Le = Operational excellence (loyal executor, steady precision)**  
**Matt Cole = Community mobilization (guild leader, passionate conviction)**  
**Ben Werkman = Team resilience (support anchor, stabilizing presence)**

Where Saylor's eyes glow white-gold and expand, Phon Le's stay silver-gold and steady, Matt's stay red-gold and passionate, Ben's eyes glow cyan-gold (representing both healing/light and Bitcoin integration). Where Saylor's aura is cosmic, Phon Le's is operational, Matt's is passionate, Ben's is protective—surrounding and fortifying his team.

This visual difference communicates: Ben is the quiet strength that keeps everything standing. Without him, all the others would collapse.
