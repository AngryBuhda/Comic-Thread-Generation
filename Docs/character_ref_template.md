## PAGE 2: FACIAL DETAILS & EXPRESSIONS

### Expression Sheet (8 Core Emotions)

For each emotion, show BOTH:
1. **Front View** - Direct facial expression
2. **3/4 Angle** - How emotion reads from side

#### Expression Categories:

**1. NEUTRAL/RESTING**
- Default expression when not in action
- Eyes: Relaxed, normal pupil
- Mouth: Closed, natural
- Brow: Not raised or furrowed
- Usage: Between scenes, dialogue moments
- Solo Leveling Note: Even resting, maintain intense gaze suggesting hidden power

**2. DETERMINED/FOCUSED**
- Shows mental resolve and strategic thinking
- Eyes: Intense, slightly narrowed
- Mouth: Clenched jaw or thin line
- Brow: Slightly furrowed or raised (depends on character)
- Usage: Before battle, making crucial decisions
- Solo Leveling Note: This is the "I'm in control" expression - eyes should have subtle glow emerging

**3. ANGRY/RAGE**
- Maximum intensity, combat aggression
- Eyes: Wide or narrowed to slits, glowing if powered
- Mouth: Snarling or teeth bared
- Brow: Deeply furrowed
- Muscles: Tensed, visible neck/shoulder strain
- Usage: During intense combat, confrontations
- Solo Leveling Note: Purple and gold aura should be visible and expanding

**4. FEARFUL/ANXIOUS**
- Shows vulnerability and stakes
- Eyes: Wide, dilated pupils
- Mouth: Slightly open or tensed
- Brow: Raised, wrinkles on forehead
- Muscles: Relaxed or ready to flee
- Usage: When facing unknown threats, before breakthrough moments
- Solo Leveling Note: Even fear should suggest hidden strength - eyes might flicker with power

**5. SURPRISED/SHOCKED**
- Immediate reaction to unexpected events
- Eyes: Maximally wide, pupils centered
- Mouth: Open in "O" shape
- Brow: Raised high
- Eyebrows: Pushed upward
- Usage: Plot twists, revelations, unexpected encounters
- Solo Leveling Note: Can quickly transition to determined as character realizes they can handle it

**6. SADNESS/DESPAIR**
- Emotional depth, character vulnerability
- Eyes: Downcast or hollow-looking
- Pupils: Often looking downward or unfocused
- Mouth: Frown or trembling
- Brow: Inner brow raised (upside-down "V")
- Usage: Character reflection, loss moments, emotional climax
- Solo Leveling Note: Aura dims or becomes unstable during despair

**7. CONFIDENT/VICTORY**
- Post-triumph, mastery, superiority
- Eyes: Smiling, narrowed contentedly
- Mouth: Slight smirk or genuine smile
- Brow: Relaxed, possibly raised slightly
- Overall: Relaxed posture, commanding presence
- Usage: After victories, when power overwhelming opponents
- Solo Leveling Note: Eyes blaze with proud power glow, aura steady and strong

**8. TRANSCENDENT/ULTIMATE**
- Beyond human emotion, accessing higher power
- Eyes: Pure glow, almost no pupil visible
- Expression: Serene, beyond fear or anger
- Mouth: Calm, possibly slight knowing smile
- Overall: Peaceful yet terrifyingly powerful
- Usage: Ultimate power moments, final form activation
- Solo Leveling Note: Character looks almost ethereal, connected to cosmic forces

### Hand Details

**Open Palm (Summoning/Power Channeling)**
- Fingers: Slightly spread, relaxed
- Palm: Glowing center, magical particles emanating
- Wrist: Shows where power flows from body
- Details: Individual finger definition, nail detail
- When to use: Summoning shadows, channeling energy, defensive posture

**Fist (Combat/Power Focus)**
- Fingers: Tightly wrapped
- Knuckles: Prominent, showing tension
- Wrist: Braced, power channeled downward
- Glow: Concentrated at knuckles if powered
- When to use: Ready stance, striking position, intense focus

**Pointing Gesture (Commanding)**
- Index finger: Extended clearly
- Other fingers: Curled naturally
- Arm: Extended outward with authority
- Detail: Shows leadership and control
- When to use: Directing shadows, ordering forces, declaration moments

**Power-Channeling Position**
- Fingers: Spread wide as if holding/shaping energy
- Palm: Center glowing brightly with runes
- Wrist: Multiple rune patterns visible
- Flow: Energy visible flowing along hand/arm
- When to use: Activating abilities, summoning, major power displays

## PAGE 3: POSE SHEET & ACTION SEQUENCES

### 6 Key Action Poses

**Pose 1: Neutral Standing**
- Purpose: Default reference pose
- Stance: Relaxed but alert, feet shoulder-width apart
- Arms: Natural position at sides
- Expression: Neutral or slightly determined
- Usage: Base reference for all other poses

**Pose 2: Combat Ready**
- Purpose: Beginning of battle, tactical positioning
- Stance: Feet spread, knees slightly bent for movement
- Arms: Raised in guard or ready position
- Expression: Intense focus, eyes glowing
- Aura: Beginning to manifest around body
- Usage: Before engaging enemies, strategic moments

**Pose 3: Summoning/Power Activation**
- Purpose: Channeling abilities, calling shadows
- Stance: Grounded, rooted position showing stability
- Arms: Extended outward, palms open, glowing
- Expression: Intense concentration
- Effects: Major glow, rune patterns visible, particles flowing
- Usage: Major ability activation scenes

**Pose 4: Mid-Strike Combat**
- Purpose: Active combat, attacking
- Stance: Lunging or pivot position showing motion
- Arms: One extended in attack, one for balance
- Expression: Angry or determined
- Effects: Speed lines, impact aura, motion blur indication
- Usage: Fight sequence panels

**Pose 5: Defensive/Protective Stance**
- Purpose: Shielding self or others
- Stance: Wide, planted firmly
- Arms: Crossed or positioned defensively
- Expression: Resolute determination
- Effects: Defensive aura/shield visible
- Usage: Protecting allies, absorbing damage

**Pose 6: Ultimate Power/Transcendence**
- Purpose: Final form activation, maximum power
- Stance: Commanding position, often with slight upward angle
- Arms: Outstretched or conducting energy
- Expression: Serene transcendence
- Effects: Massive aura filling frame, cosmic particles, intense glow
- Usage: Epic final moments, power climax

### Pose Consistency Notes
- [ ] Head remains recognizable across all poses
- [ ] Body proportions maintain consistency
- [ ] Clothing fits naturally in each position
- [ ] Joints bend realistically
- [ ] Weight distribution appears balanced
- [ ] Character feels like same person in all poses

### Materials & Textures

**Fabric Materials**
- **Matte Cloth:** Absorbs light, no shine, realistic fabric
- **Glossy Leather:** Reflects some light, smooth appearance
- **Metallic Armor:** Highly reflective, shows environment
- **Ethereal/Magical:** Translucent or glow-based, particles visible

**Texture Application Guide**
- Cotton/Cloth: Soft shadows, subtle wrinkles
- Leather: Uniform sheen with defined creases
- Metal: Strong highlights and shadows, sharp reflections
- Magic: Soft glow edges, particle effects, flowing appearance

### Glow Effects Specifications

**Aura Visualization (Solo Leveling Style)**

**Tier 1 (Base Form)**
- Aura Size: None or 0.1x body size
- Color: None visible
- Glow Intensity: 0%
- Particle Density: None
- When visible: Only during extreme concentration

**Tier 2 (Awakening)**
- Aura Size: 0.5x body size
- Color: Subtle gold/purple blend
- Glow Intensity: 20-30%
- Particle Density: Minimal particles
- Effect: Soft glow around shoulders/head

**Tier 3 (Powered Form)**
- Aura Size: 1-2x body size
- Color: Bright gold and purple
- Glow Intensity: 60-80%
- Particle Density: Medium, rune-like particles
- Effect: Clear visible aura with defined edges

**Tier 4 (Ultimate Form)**
- Aura Size: 3-5x body size filling frame
- Color: White-gold with purple corona
- Glow Intensity: 95-100%
- Particle Density: Heavy, cosmic particles
- Effect: Transcendent glow, light bleeds onto surroundings

**Rune Pattern Progression**

**When Runes Appear**
- Tier 1: No visible runes
- Tier 2: Subtle patterns on shoulders (10% opacity)
- Tier 3: Clear patterns on arms and chest (40% opacity)
- Tier 4: Full body coverage with intense glow (80-100% opacity)

**Rune Design**
- Style: Geometric, flowing curves, magical aesthetic
- Color: Gold (#FFD700) with purple (#9D00FF) accents
- Animation: Glow pulses with character's power level
- Placement: Follows natural body armor locations (shoulders, forearms, chest, legs)
