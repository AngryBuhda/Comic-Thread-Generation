# Character Archetypes & Stakeholders Guide
## Bitcoin Narrative Made Personal: Who They Are, What They Represent, How They Look

## INTRODUCTION: CHARACTER AS THESIS

In this story, **characters are not individuals—they are concepts made visible**.

- **Michael Saylor** = Infinite Duration Understanding incarnate
- **STRK, STRF, STRD** = Bitcoin believers mobilized as legendary warriors
- **Skeptic Collective** = Organized doubt
- **S&P Mecha** = Institutional blindness enforced mechanically

This guide ensures every character's visual design communicates their narrative role instantly.
