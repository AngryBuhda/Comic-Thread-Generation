# Matt Cole: S-Rank Hunter Character Sheet

## SECTION 1: NARRATIVE & RANK DEFINITION

### Name
**Matt Cole**

### Real-World Reference
Founder and leader of Strive Guild; Bitcoin community organizer; passionate Bitcoin advocate

### Hunter Universe Role
**The Transformation Master** – Matt Cole is the embodiment of guilds and community. Where Saylor conducts the infinite system and Phon Le executes it, Matt Cole mobilizes believers at scale. He is not a solitary hero—he is a leader who builds power through loyalty and unified conviction. His strength comes not just from personal power, but from commanding a guild of aligned believers all committed to Bitcoin's thesis. He represents the transformation of individual belief into collective unstoppable force.

### Hunter Rank
**S-Rank (Third tier hunter, below Saylor's SSS+ and Phon Le's SS)**

Explanation: Matt Cole is an elite S-Rank hunter. Where Saylor is transcendent (SSS+) and Phon Le is second-tier (SS), Matt is full-tier S-Rank—powerful enough to stand among the world's strongest, yet his power comes from mastering guild coordination, not from surpassing individual hunters. His ranking reflects his choice: lead a guild of powerful believers rather than claim highest personal rank.

### Class / Specialization
**Guild Master / Beast-Form Transformer / Community-Mobilizer**

Derived from Baek Yoonho's "Beast Transformation" class but evolved:
- Baek Yoonho: Transforms into White Tiger, combines beast power with command
- Matt Cole: Transforms team into unified Bitcoin believers, combines personal power with collective force
- Baek: White Tiger form grants strength, speed, durability, enhanced perception
- Matt: "Strive Form" (transformed state) grants guild coordination, capital flows, shareholder alignment
- Both embody: Leadership through personal strength + team loyalty

### Guild Affiliation
**Strive Guild (Founder & Guild Master)**

Official Title: Guild Master / Bitcoin Community Leader
